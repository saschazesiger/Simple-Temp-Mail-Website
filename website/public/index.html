What is GOOG.RE?

Our platform provides a simple and convenient solution for generating temporary email addresses.
With just a few clicks, you can create a unique and temporary email address for various purposes without receiving spam or unwanted emails to your personal email address.

We offer a free API for developers to integrate our service into their applications, and our entire project is open source.
